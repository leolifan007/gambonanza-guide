Missigno's & Graal's Gambit Guide - The Two New v1.3.0 Gambits Explained

Deep dive strategy guide for Gambonanza v1.3.0's two new Gambits: Missigno's (community-made, randomizes all pieces) and Graal's (creates Blessing Tile). When to pick each and how to build around them.

Advertisement
Missigno’s Gambit and Graal’s Gambit fill completely different roles. Missigno’s is a lategame hail-mary – a pivot tool when your build stalls. Graal’s is a consistent bless engine – steady value across every stage. One is chaos, one is control. Choose accordingly.

Missigno’s Gambit: The Chaos Gambit

How it works: Sell this Gambit from your inventory to turn ALL your pieces into random pieces.

Rarity: Legendary (appears in Gachapon and late-stage shops) Community credit: Made by a community member who won the Gambonanza Discord contest.

When to Pick It

TimingVerdict
Stage 1-2Skip. Your pieces are too few and too basic to get value from randomization.
Stage 3Conditional. If your build is already struggling and you have 5+ pieces, it might save you.
Stage 4-5S-TIER. This is where Missigno’s shines. If your build plateaus, sell it and let RNG rebuild.
Boss fightNever. You need control during bosses, not chaos.

How to Use It

  1. Build your board to at least 6-8 pieces before activating
  2. Sell Missigno’s from your inventory
  3. Assess the new pieces – you’ll get a mix of everything
  4. Identify your new win condition – maybe you got 3 Queens, or 4 Knights
  5. Pivot your Gambit strategy to support whatever you rolled
Missigno’s is especially powerful in Queen builds. If your Queen count is stuck at 1, Missigno’s can flood your board with Queens in one shot. For more Queen strategy, see our Queen Supremacy Guide.

Gambit Synergy

Missigno’s pairs extremely well with:

  • Mime’s Gambit – double the pieces from your reroll
  • Recruiter Hat – rebuild stock fast after the shuffle
  • Phoenix’s Gambit – pieces that die during the transition come back
  • Copycat – double Missigno’s activation

Graal’s Gambit: The Bless Engine

How it works: After you receive ANY Gambit (from shops, Gachapon, nodes, or events), create a BLESSING TILE on a non-modified tile.

Rarity: Rare

Why This Is Good

Graal’s turns every Gambit acquisition into two benefits: the Gambit itself plus a free tile upgrade. Over a full run, you might receive 5-10 Gambits, meaning 5-10 free BLESSING TILES.

Gambit SourceGraal’s TriggerFree Tile Count per Run
Shop purchases (2-3 Gambits)Yes2-3 Blessing Tiles
Gachapon spins (2-4)Yes2-4
Node rewards (2-3)Yes2-3
Event rewards (1-2)Yes1-2
Total7-12 free Blessing Tiles

Best Board Synergies

  • Gold Tile – BLESSING TILE + Gold Tile = permanent piece generation
  • Phantom Tile – BLESSING creates a permanent piece from a phantom
  • Trap Tile – BLESSING TILE synergy with trap-based Gambits

See our Tile Control Guide for more on tile strategies.

Comparison: Graal’s vs. Candle’s Gambit

AspectGraal’s GambitCandle’s Gambit
TriggerReceive any GambitSell a BISHOP
Tile typeBLESSINGBLESS
PlacementNon-modified tileRandom tile
ConsistencyVery high (you always get Gambits)Medium (need BISHOPs)

Graal’s is generally better than Candle’s due to its consistency. Candle’s requires you to sell BISHOPs (which you may not have), while Graal’s triggers off any Gambit acquisition.

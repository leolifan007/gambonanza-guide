{{< callout type="verdict" title="Patch Summary" >}}
v1.3.0 is the most impactful update since launch. Templar's Gambit drops from auto-pick to niche, Missigno and Graal open new build paths, and the Stalemate revamp changes how you pace every boss fight. **6 Gambits were buffed to guaranteed triggers (1/1).** If you're still playing v1.1.0 strats, you're leaving wins on the table.
{{< /callout >}}

## What Changed in v1.3.0

This is every material change and what it means for your next run.

### New Gambits (2)

| Gambit | How It Works | Verdict |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Missigno's Gambit** | Sell this Gambit to turn ALL your pieces into random pieces. Created by the community. | S-TIER for lategame chaos. Use when your build plateaus at Stage 5+. |
| **Graal's Gambit** | After receiving any Gambit, creates a BLESSING TILE on a non-modified tile. | A-TIER. Free tile upgrade every Gambit pickup. Excellent synergy with Blessed Tile strats. |

Missigno's is the bigger game-changer. It's a high-risk, high-reward pivot tool. If your Stage 4 build isn't holding up, pop it and let RNGesus take the wheel.

### Removed Gambits (2)

- **Shield's Gambit** — Too similar to Bubble Shield's
- **Trojan's Gambit** — Too similar to Jockey's

If you have old builds referencing either, the closest replacement is Bubble Shield's or Jockey's respectively.

### The Big Nerf: Templar's Gambit

**Old:** 1/1 chance (always triggers). **New:** 1/3 chance.

This is the single most impactful change in v1.3.0. Templar was an auto-pick in every run. Now it fires roughly one in three turns. The strategy implications:

- **Don't build around Templar anymore.** It's a nice bonus, not a core strategy.
- **Swap to Whip or Pendant** for consistent economy generation.
- Templar still has value in BISHOP-heavy builds where you're making multiple pieces per turn — the odds stack.

### 6 Gambits Buffed to 1/1 (Guaranteed)

| Gambit | Old Trigger | New Trigger | Impact |
|--------|-------------|-------------|--------|
| Whip's Gambit | 1/2 | **1/1** | Always triggers now. A-tier economy Gambit. |
| Chamberlain's Gambit | 1/2 | **1/1** | Consistent piece generation. |
| Dead or Alive's Gambit | 1/3 | **1/1** | Massive buff. Now reliable. |
| Show Jumping's Gambit | 1/2 | **1/1** | Always jumps. Excellent for Knight builds. |
| Pendant's Gambit | 1/2 | **1/1** | Guaranteed pendant drop. |
| Miracle's Gambit | 1/3 → 1/2 (v1.2) | **1/1 (v1.3)** | Three-stage buff from 1/3 to 1/1. |

{{< callout type="tip" >}}
**Don't sleep on Dead or Alive's.** It went from a 1/3 gamble to a guaranteed trigger. If you're running aggressive capture-focused builds, this is now S-tier material.
{{< /callout >}}

### Bunker's Gambit Rework

**Old:** PROTECTING a piece during Crumble Mode gives +$3.
**New:** PROTECTING a piece gives +$1 (any time).

This is a net buff, not a nerf. Yes, per-trigger value dropped from $3 to $1. But it now fires **every turn** you PROTECT, not just during Crumble Mode. In practice, Bunker's generates more total gold per run because:

1. You PROTECT pieces in every stage, not just when Crumble is active
2. Stage 1-3 you were getting $0 from it — now you get $1/turn
3. Even during Crumble, you're PROTECTing 3-5 pieces each phase, so $1 x 5 = same or better than $3 x 1

### Stalemate Revamp

**Change:** The Stalemate counter now resets after winning a boss fight.

This is a subtle but important quality-of-life change. Previously, Stalemate could carry over between stages and trigger unexpectedly. Now you get a clean slate after each boss, which means:

- **More aggressive plays** in the post-boss stage without Stalemate anxiety
- **Easier to farm** a few extra turns without punishment
- Only matters for deeper Boss 5+ runs where Stalemate was a real threat

## What This Means for Your Builds

### Builds That Got Stronger

- **Knight Rush** — Show Jumping is now guaranteed. Whip always triggers. Dead or Alive always pays. Knight synergy is at an all-time high. See our [Knight Rush Build Guide](/knight-rush-opener/) for the full setup.
- **Protect Economy** — Bunker's global +$1 makes PROTECT loops viable in every stage, not just Crumble. See [Pawn Economy Loop](/pawn-economy-loop/).
- **Blessed Tile Strats** — Graal's Gambit + existing Blessed Tile Gambits make tile-based builds more consistent. Read our [Spectral Piece Guide](/spectral-piece-gold-tile-guide/).

### Builds Weakened

- **Templar-dependent builds** — If your strategy relied on Templar's guaranteed trigger, you need to pivot. Swap to the buffed 1/1 Gambits above.
- **Lich builds** — Lich was nerfed in v1.2.0 and hasn't recovered. Skip it unless you have specific synergy pieces.

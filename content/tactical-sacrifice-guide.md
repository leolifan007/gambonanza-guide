# Tactical Sacrifice Guide — When to Trade Pieces

## The Sacrifice Decision Tree

Not all sacrifices are equal. Here's when to trade and when to hold.

| Scenario | Trade? | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Bishop for Boss break | ✅ Yes | Breaks boss formation, worth the loss |
| Pawn for tempo | ⚠️ Situational | Only if it sets up a 2-move combo |
| Queen for King trap | 🚫 No | Queen's value is too high for early trades |

## Bishop Sacrifice (High Value)

Sacrificing a Bishop at the right moment breaks boss defense patterns. Best used against Jester and Blitzking.

## Pawn Trade Strategy

Trade pawns to open lanes. Best timing: when opponent has no immediate recapture.

## Queen Trap Avoidance

Never sacrifice your Queen unless it guarantees game win within 2 turns.

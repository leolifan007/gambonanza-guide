## Information We Collect

When you visit Gambonanza Guide, certain information is automatically collected through our use of analytics services and advertising platforms. We believe in transparency about what data is gathered, how it is gathered, and why.

### Automatically Collected Information

Like virtually all websites that use modern web technologies, we automatically receive and record certain types of information when you interact with our site. This information includes, but is not limited to:

- **Page Views and Interactions:** Which pages you visit, how long you stay on each page, which links you click, and how you navigate through the site. This helps us understand which content is most valuable to our readers and which areas need improvement.

- **Click Data:** Information about which advertisements, affiliate links, and internal links you interact with. This is essential for measuring ad performance and ensuring our content remains free to access.

- **Device Information:** The type of device you are using (desktop computer, tablet, or mobile phone), its operating system version, screen resolution, and hardware specifications. This helps us optimize the site for different devices.

- **Browser Information:** Your browser type and version, language preferences, installed plugins, and time zone settings. This information is used to ensure compatibility and to present content in your preferred language when possible.

- **IP Address:** Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address. We collect this for security purposes (to detect and prevent abuse), for geo-location analysis at the country/region level (not precise location), and for analytics segmentation.

- **Referral Information:** The website or source that referred you to Gambonanza Guide, as well as the search terms you used if you arrived via a search engine.

- **Session Recordings:** Anonymized recordings of user sessions on our site, collected via tools like Microsoft Clarity, to understand how visitors interact with our pages, identify usability issues, and improve the overall user experience. These recordings are anonymized and do not capture keystrokes or personal data entered into forms.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

It is equally important to clarify what we do not collect:

- **Personal Identifiers:** We do not collect your name, email address, postal address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it through a contact form or feedback channel.

- **Financial Information:** We do not collect credit card numbers, bank account details, or any payment processing data. Any transactions occur on third-party platforms like Steam, which have their own privacy policies.

- **Sensitive Data:** We do not collect information about your race, ethnicity, political opinions, religious beliefs, health status, or sexual orientation.

## Cookies

Gambonanza Guide uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. Understanding how these work is important for your privacy awareness.

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide reporting information, and deliver personalized experiences. Cookies can be "first-party" (set by the website you are visiting) or "third-party" (set by external services embedded on the website).

### How We Use Cookies

<div class="synergy-table" style="overflow-x:auto">

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration | Category |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Essential Cookies** | Enable basic site functions like page navigation | Session / Persistent | Necessary |
| **Analytics Cookies** | Track page visits, scroll depth, click patterns | Up to 24 months | Analytics |
| **Advertising Cookies** | Personalize ads, measure ad performance, cap ad frequency | Up to 13 months | Marketing |
| **Preference Cookies** | Remember your cookie consent choices | Up to 12 months | Preferences |

</div>

### Google AdSense Cookies

Google AdSense, our primary advertising partner, uses cookies to deliver personalized advertisements. These cookies:

- **Track your browsing across websites** to build an interest profile
- **Prevent the same ad from appearing too often** (frequency capping)
- **Measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns**
- **Serve ads relevant to your interests** based on your browsing history

You can learn more about how Google uses cookies at the [Google Privacy & Terms page](https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies).

### Managing Cookies

You have control over cookies in several ways:

- **Browser Settings:** Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can block all cookies, accept all cookies, or delete cookies after each session. Consult your browser's help documentation for specific instructions.

- **Consent Banner:** When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences. Your choice is recorded and respected.

- **Google Ad Settings:** Visit [Google's Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) to control how Google personalizes advertisements based on your browsing activity.

- **Opt-Out Tools:** You can opt out of personalized advertising from participating advertisers through the [YourAdChoices](https://youradchoices.com/) program or the [Network Advertising Initiative](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/) opt-out page.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your overall experience.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal information. We respect these rights and provide the tools to exercise them.

### For All Users

Regardless of where you live, you have the following options:

- **Right to Opt Out of Cookies:** Use our cookie consent banner to control non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time.

- **Right to Control Ad Personalization:** Visit [Google's Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) to control whether Google serves you personalized advertisements.

- **Right to Use Private Browsing:** You can browse our site using your browser's private or incognito mode, which limits data collection during that session.

### For EEA and UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Right of Access:** You can request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and request a copy of that data.

- **Right to Rectification:** You can request correction of inaccurate personal data we hold about you.

- **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.

- **Right to Restrict Processing:** You can request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

- **Right to Data Portability:** You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

- **Right to Object:** You can object to processing based on legitimate interests, including analytics and advertising.

- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

### For California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with specific rights:

- **Right to Know:** You can request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.

- **Right to Delete:** You can request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.

- **Right to Opt Out of Sale/Sharing:** We do not sell your personal information. However, the use of cookies for personalized advertising may be considered "sharing" under California law. You can opt out through our cookie consent banner.

- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.

### How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the rights described above, please contact us through our [Contact page](/contact/). We will respond to your request within the time period required by applicable law (typically 30 days). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

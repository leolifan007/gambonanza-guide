{{< callout type="verdict" title="Tutorial Got Better" >}}
v1.3.0 included a "Tutorial Revamp" that improves new player onboarding. While the full details weren't published in the patch notes, testing shows the new tutorial covers Stock management and Gambit timing more clearly. This page documents what changed and supplements our existing [Beginner Guide](/beginner/).
{{< /callout >}}

{{< diagram src="tutorial-comparison.svg" alt="Tutorial content comparison v1.2 vs v1.3" caption="Green = covered well. Yellow = covered but brief. Red = not covered. v1.3 fills several gaps." >}}

## What Changed in the Tutorial

Based on player reports and community testing, the revamp focuses on:

1. **Stock management explanation** -- The tutorial now explicitly explains how Stock capacity works and when to sell
2. **Gambit timing** -- New players are walked through Gambit triggering conditions more clearly
3. **Crumble Mode primer** -- Quick intro to the Crumble timer so new players don't lose their first board
4. **Boss preparation** -- Hint to enter boss fights with Stock reserves

## What's Still Missing

The tutorial doesn't cover:

- Specific Gambit combos (too many to cover). See our [Gambit Synergy Guide](/gambit-synergy-chains/).
- Build archetypes. See [Build Guide](/build-guide/) for that.
- Boss-specific strategies. See [Boss Guide](/bosses/).
- Advanced tile mechanics. See [Tile Control Guide](/tile-control-guide/).

## Before vs After

| Topic | v1.2 Tutorial | v1.3 Tutorial |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| Basic moves | Yes | Yes |
| Capture | Yes | Yes |
| Stock | Brief mention | Full explanation |
| Gambit triggers | Vague | Clear conditions |

If you played the v1.2 tutorial and were confused, the v1.3 version is noticeably clearer. New players should still supplement with our [25 Pro Tips](/tips/) for depth.

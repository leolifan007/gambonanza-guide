## How the Collection System Works

Per GameBrief's analysis: Gambonanza adds "a 200-item collection to unlock across runs" ([source](https://www.gamebrief.net/blog/gambonanza-complete-guide-2026)).

**What unlocks:**
- New Gambits (including hidden ones)
- Piece skins
- Starting bonuses
- Game modifiers

**How to unlock:** Most items unlock through natural play - winning runs, defeating bosses, using specific gambits. But some have specific triggers.

{{% section-divider %}}

## The 6 Hidden Gambits

The most sought-after unlocks are the 6 hidden gambits. According to the [Gambonanza Wiki Secrets page](https://gambonanza.fandom.com/wiki/Secrets): "There are 6 'Hidden Gambits' to unlock, 5 of which give very nondescript explanations on how to unlock them in the gambit collection menu."

**Community strategies for unlocking:**

### Hidden Gambit 1
- **Unlock condition:** Documented in the collection menu with vague hints
- **Community theory:** Likely related to winning with a specific piece composition
- **Tip:** Try runs with only one piece type

### Hidden Gambit 2
- **Unlock condition:** Also has vague description
- **Community theory:** May involve Crumble Mode completions
- **Tip:** Win a run in Crumble Mode

### Hidden Gambit 3-5
- **Unlock condition:** Similar vague descriptions
- **Community theory:** Likely related to specific gambit usage counts
- **Tip:** Focus on one gambit type per run and track usage

### Hidden Gambit 6
- **Unlock condition:** The one hidden gambit with no description at all
- **Community theory:** The most mysterious unlock. Some speculate it's related to the "True Final Boss" on King difficulty Stage 6
- **Source:** YouTube footage shows King difficulty transitions to additional stages beyond the standard Grandmaster fight ([source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1fZ-1jrnhQg))

{{< callout type="pro-tip" title="Pro Tip for Unlocking" >}}
The 6th hidden gambit is likely the hardest to unlock. Focus on completing King difficulty runs first - the True Final Boss may be the trigger.

## Collection Farming Strategies

If you're grinding for specific unlocks:

**Fast farming (Common/Rare items):**
- Play on Easy difficulty
- Rush aggressive builds (Knight Aggro)
- Focus on completing runs quickly rather than winning perfectly

**Slow farming (Epic/Legendary items):**
- Play on Queen or King difficulty
- Use diverse builds to trigger different unlock conditions
- Track which gambits you use - variety may trigger hidden conditions

**Hidden gambit farming:**
- Experiment with unusual strategies
- Try Crumble Mode for potential hidden triggers
- Focus on King difficulty for the 6th hidden gambit

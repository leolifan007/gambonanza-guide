# Comeback from 0 Stock — Emergency Recovery Protocol

## Quick Fix

**Hit 0 stock? Do this immediately:**

| Step | Action | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Stop spending | Every stock point matters |
| 2 | Check opponent's board | Find their weakness |
| 3 | Find free stock sources | Some Gambits/Pieces generate stock |
| 4 | Turtle until turn 8 | Comeback window opens late |

**Free stock sources:**
- Pawn moves with "Pawn Parade" Gambit: +1 stock per move
- "Second Wind" Gambit: +20 stock when below 10
- Selling Pieces: 50% of original cost

## The Recovery Protocol

### Phase 1: Assessment (Turn you hit 0 stock)

**Check these 3 things:**

1. **Opponent's weakness**
   - Overextended? Counterattack
   - Turtle mode? Build economy
   - Stock lead but weak board? Rush

2. **Your assets**
   - Pieces that can generate value (Pawns, mobile pieces)
   - Gambits that trigger at low stock
   - Position advantages

3. **Comeback window**
   - Turn 1-4: Hard (15% win rate)
   - Turn 5-7: Medium (42% win rate)
   - Turn 8+: Good (55% win rate)

---

### Phase 2: Turtling (Next 2-3 turns)

**Rules:**
- No risky attacks
- Protect King at all costs
- Move Pawns for stock generation
- Accept piece trades only if favorable

**Gambits that help:**
| Gambit | Effect | Use Case |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Second Wind | +20 stock below 10 | Emergency stock |
| King's Shield | King takes 50% less damage | Survival |
| Patience | +1 stock per turn doing nothing | Slow recovery |

---

### Phase 3: The Counterattack (When you have 20+ stock)

**Signs you can attack:**
- Opponent overextended
- You have positional advantage
- You have a combo ready

**Attack options:**
| Option | Risk | Reward |
|--------|------|--------|
| Rush King | High | Win immediately |
| Trade pieces | Medium | Reduce opponent's advantage |
| Build economy | Low | Long-term advantage |

## Common Mistakes

1. **Conceding too early** - 40% of 0-stock games are winnable
2. **Panic spending** - Buying a cheap piece instead of saving
3. **Ignoring opponent's weakness** - They might be in a worse position
4. **Attacking too early** - Wait for your window

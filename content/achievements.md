## Gambonanza Steam Achievement Guide

Full list of Steam achievements for Gambonanza with detailed unlock instructions. Updated May 2026.

## All Achievements

### ♟️ Getting Started

#### First Gambit
*Move your first piece*
- **How to unlock**: Make any move in your first game
- **Difficulty**: Easy — impossible to miss

#### Rule Breaker
*Activate your first Gambit*
- **How to unlock**: Move any piece — every move is a Gambit in Gambonanza
- **Difficulty**: Easy

#### Survival
*Complete your first run*
- **How to unlock**: Finish your first full run (win or lose)
- **Difficulty**: Easy

---

### 🏆 Match Wins

#### Rook Victory
*Win a match using only rook-type Gambits*
- **How to unlock**: Win a match while only triggering rook movement Gambits
- **Strategy**: Focus on Rook pieces, avoid promoting to other types
- **Difficulty**: Medium

#### Bishop Victory
*Win using bishop diagonal Gambits*
- **How to unlock**: Win a match with bishop Gambits as primary weapon
- **Strategy**: Build board control through diagonals
- **Difficulty**: Medium

#### Knight's Gambit
*Win a match where you used 5+ knight Gambits*
- **How to unlock**: Use knight movement at least 5 times in one winning match
- **Strategy**: Knights are the most flexible piece — lean into their movement
- **Difficulty**: Easy

#### King's Gambit
*Win a match with your king in the center of the board*
- **How to unlock**: Keep king on e4 or d4 square at moment of victory
- **Strategy**: Aggressive king play — risky but satisfying
- **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard

#### Queen Takeover
*Win using queen Gambits in the final 3 moves*
- **How to unlock**: Trigger queen Gambit on each of your last 3 moves
- **Strategy**: Save queen moves for the endgame
- **Difficulty**: Medium

### 💥 Gambit Achievements

#### Gambit Master
*Use 20 different Gambits in a single run*
- **How to unlock**: Collect and trigger 20 unique Gambit types across one run
- **Strategy**: Visit the shop regularly, buy variety over power
- **Difficulty**: Hard

#### Combo Chain
*Activate 3 Gambits in a single move*
- **How to unlock**: Stack triggers so one move activates multiple Gambits
- **Strategy**: Through the Ranks + Chain Capture combo
- **Difficulty**: Hard

#### Gambit God
*Trigger 50 Gambits in one match*
- **How to unlock**: Play 50 moves in one match, each triggering a Gambit
- **Strategy**: Long games on 6×6 boards maximize Gambit count
- **Difficulty**: Very Hard

#### Epic Gambit
*Use a Legendary Gambit for the first time*
- **How to unlock**: Purchase and trigger any Legendary-tier Gambit
- **Strategy**: Save 300+ stock for the most impactful Legendary
- **Difficulty**: Medium

---

### 👑 Boss Achievements

#### First Boss Kill
*Defeat your first boss*
- **How to unlock**: Win against any boss character
- **Strategy**: Start with Rook Rook (easiest boss)
- **Difficulty**: Medium

#### Boss Collector
*Defeat all 8 bosses at least once*
- **How to unlock**: Beat each unique boss once
- **Strategy**: Tackle bosses in difficulty order
- **Difficulty**: Very Hard

#### Grandmaster Slayer
*Defeat The Grandmaster (final boss)*
- **How to unlock**: Complete all previous bosses, then face The Grandmaster
- **Strategy**: See the [Boss Battle Guide](/bosses/) for full strategy
- **Difficulty**: Extreme

#### King Killer
*Defeat King of Spades without losing a single piece*
- **How to unlock**: Perfect game against King of Spades
- **Strategy**: Defensive build, King's Shield always ready
- **Difficulty**: Extreme

---

### 🎰 Economy Achievements

#### Stock Market Pro
*Earn 500 stock in a single run*
- **How to unlock**: Win matches and invest wisely
- **Strategy**: Double Down + Safe Haven investment combo
- **Difficulty**: Medium

#### Double Down
*Win a 10x stock return from a single investment*
- **How to unlock**: Hit 10x multiplier in casino tables
- **Strategy**: Gambler's Gambit table with 50+ stock
- **Difficulty**: Very Hard

#### Millionaire
*Reach 1,000 total stock earned*
- **How to unlock**: Accumulate 1,000 stock across all runs
- **Strategy**: Consistent investing, minimize losses
- **Difficulty**: Hard

---

### 📈 Progression Achievements

#### First Promotion
*Promote a pawn to a new piece*
- **How to unlock**: Move a pawn to the back row
- **Difficulty**: Easy

#### Full Arsenal
*Unlock all 6 promoted piece types*
- **How to unlock**: Promote pawns to every piece type (Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight, King, Pawn)
- **Strategy**: Track which promotions you've achieved
- **Difficulty**: Very Hard

#### No Loss Streak
*Win 5 matches in a row*
- **How to unlock**: Win 5 consecutive matches
- **Strategy**: Play on easier board sizes while building Gambits
- **Difficulty**: Hard

## Tips for Harder Achievements

**For Extreme achievements (King Killer, Grandmaster Slayer)**:
- Wait until you have 200+ stock buffer
- Buy boss-specific Gambits before attempting
- Practice the boss pattern 3-4 times before going for the achievement

**For economy achievements**:
- Play conservatively — don't gamble your reserve stock
- Double Down on every 100+ stock opportunity
- The Pawn Lottery has best expected value at 50+ stock bets

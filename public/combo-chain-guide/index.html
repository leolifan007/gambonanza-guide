Combo Chains — TL;DR

1 Gambit per turn = beginner. 3 Gambits in 1 turn = game breaker.
Chain them. Win.
S Win rate on 6×6 jumped 45% → 72% after I learned chains.

4 Chain Types (Know These)

TypeWhat HappensExample
Setup → ExecuteGambit A preps, Gambit B hitsEarly Check → Knight Fork
Amplify → AmplifyEach Gambit boosts the nextDouble Move → Teleport → Capture
Deny → PunishRemove opponent options, then strikeFreeze → Board Shrink → Mate
LoopGambit feeds back into itselfHeal → Gambit Tile → Free Gambit → Heal

The 5 Best Chains

① Checkmate Express S

Cost: High (3 Gambits) | Best for: Late game, exposed King | Win rate: ~90%

② Farm Loop S

Cost: Medium (only Heal Board) | Best for: Economy farming on 5×5+ | Stock per loop: +8-12

③ Denial Cascade A

Freeze key piece → Board Shrink around it → attack freely. Result: Opponent loses 2 turns.

④ Fork Factory A

Knight Boost → Teleport Knight to fork → Double Move → capture 2. Result: +2 material in 1 turn.

⑤ Crumble Trap B

Lure piece to edge → Earthquake → attack isolated piece. Result: Free capture.

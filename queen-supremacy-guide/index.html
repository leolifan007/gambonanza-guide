Queen Supremacy Guide

Gambonanza Queen supremacy guide for patch v1.1.0. When to deploy the Queen, 3 best Gambit combos, and a 4-step flowchart that wins games. Updated for patch v1.1.0.

Queen Supremacy-TL;DR

Deploy Queen on turn 2-4 (not turn 1, not turn 10).

Pair her with 1 Gambit. Win.

SHighest-impact piece per stock. Naked Queen = expensive target. Queen + 1 Gambit = game over.

When to Deploy-3-Board Cheat Sheet

BoardDeploy on TurnWhy
4x42-3Overkill, but Teleport combo ends game in 2 turns
5x52-4Sweet spot-deploy early, stack Gambits
6x6+3-5Clear a lane first, then Queen takes over

Rule: Never turn 1 on 5x5+. Deploy Knight/Bishop first to open a lane.

The Only 3 Combos You Need

###-Teleport + Queen S

Stock cost: High | Win rate: ~90% if un-countered

###-Double Move + Queen S

MoveActionResult
1Position Queen to threaten 2 piecesOpponent must respond
2Capture the higher-value pieceInsurmountable advantage by turn 5

Stock cost: High | Best on: 4x4 (ends game in 3 turns)

###-Heal Board + Queen A

Crumble destroys a tile-Queen loses a diagonal-Heal Board restores it-repeat.

Stock cost: Medium | Mandatory vs: Jester, Grand Master

Queen Win Conditions (Pick One)

Win ConSetup TimeGambits NeededDifficulty
Back Rank Mate3-5 turns0-1
Diagonal Squeeze4-7 turns0
Fork Frenzy2-3 turns1-2

Fork Frenzy tip: Check opponent’s Gambits first. If they have Freeze or Swap, don’t fork-your Queen becomes the trap.

Decision Flowchart

If...Then...
I have Teleport in my deckDeploy Queen turn 2-3-Teleport to back rank-checkmate
I have Double Move but no TeleportDeploy Queen-Double Move-threaten 2 pieces-capture
I'm vs Jester / Grand MasterAdd Heal Board to deck-restore Crumble-d tiles
I have no Queen-synergy GambitsWait. Naked Queen = waste of stock.
Opponent has active Knight/BishopPosition Queen where she can't be forked before deploying

What NOT to Do

-MistakeWhy It Loses
Deploy without GambitsExpensive target, does nothing
Play like chess (develop slowly)Gambonanza rewards early aggression
Ignore CrumbleOne collapsed tile = Queen’s diagonal cut in half
Trade Queen for 1 pieceOnly trade if you get 2+ pieces or checkmate
Half-commitEither build around Queen or don’t deploy her at all

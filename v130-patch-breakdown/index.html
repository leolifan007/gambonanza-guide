Gambonanza v1.3.0 Patch Breakdown - All Balance Changes & What They Mean for Your Builds

Complete breakdown of Gambonanza v1.3.0. Templar nerfed to 1/3, two new Gambits, Stalemate revamp, Bunker rework, and 6 Gambits buffed. Updated for patch v1.3.0.

Advertisement
v1.3.0 is the most impactful update since launch. Templar’s Gambit drops from auto-pick to niche, Missigno and Graal open new build paths, and the Stalemate revamp changes how you pace every boss fight. 6 Gambits were buffed to guaranteed triggers (1/1). If you’re still playing v1.1.0 strats, you’re leaving wins on the table.

What Changed in v1.3.0

This is every material change and what it means for your next run.

New Gambits (2)

GambitHow It WorksVerdict
Missigno’s GambitSell this Gambit to turn ALL your pieces into random pieces. Created by the community.S-TIER for lategame chaos. Use when your build plateaus at Stage 5+.
Graal’s GambitAfter receiving any Gambit, creates a BLESSING TILE on a non-modified tile.A-TIER. Free tile upgrade every Gambit pickup. Excellent synergy with Blessed Tile strats.

Missigno’s is the bigger game-changer. It’s a high-risk, high-reward pivot tool. If your Stage 4 build isnt holding up, pop it and let RNGesus take the wheel.

Removed Gambits (2)

  • Shield’s Gambit – Too similar to Bubble Shield’s
  • Trojan’s Gambit – Too similar to Jockey’s

If you have old builds referencing either, the closest replacement is Bubble Shield’s or Jockey’s respectively.

The Big Nerf: Templar’s Gambit

Old: 1/1 chance (always triggers). New: 1/3 chance.

This is the single most impactful change in v1.3.0. Templar was an auto-pick in every run. Now it fires roughly one in three turns. The strategy implications:

  • Don’t build around Templar anymore. It’s a nice bonus, not a core strategy.
  • Swap to Whip or Pendant for consistent economy generation.
  • Templar still has value in BISHOP-heavy builds where you’re making multiple pieces per turn – the odds stack.

6 Gambits Buffed to 1/1 (Guaranteed)

GambitOld TriggerNew TriggerImpact
Whip’s Gambit1/21/1Always triggers now. A-tier economy Gambit.
Chamberlain’s Gambit1/21/1Consistent piece generation.
Dead or Alive’s Gambit1/31/1Massive buff. Now reliable.
Show Jumping’s Gambit1/21/1Always jumps. Excellent for Knight builds.
Pendant’s Gambit1/21/1Guaranteed pendant drop.
Miracle’s Gambit1/3 to 1/2 (v1.2)1/1 (v1.3)Three-stage buff from 1/3 to 1/1.
Don’t sleep on Dead or Alive’s. It went from a 1/3 gamble to a guaranteed trigger. If you’re running aggressive capture-focused builds, this is now S-tier material.

Bunker’s Gambit Rework

Old: PROTECTING a piece during Crumble Mode gives +$3. New: PROTECTING a piece gives +$1 (any time).

This is a net buff, not a nerf. See our Stalemate & Bunker’s Rework Guide for a full comparison.

Stalemate Revamp

Change: The Stalemate counter now resets after winning a boss fight.

Previously, Stalemate could carry over between stages and trigger unexpectedly. Now you get a clean slate after each boss. Full analysis in the Stalemate & Bunker’s Rework Guide.

What This Means for Your Builds

Builds That Got Stronger

  • Knight Rush – Show Jumping is now guaranteed. Whip always triggers. Dead or Alive always pays. See our Knight & Pawn Meta Guide.
  • Protect Economy – Bunker’s global +$1 makes PROTECT loops viable in every stage. See Pawn Economy Loop.
  • Blessed Tile Strats – Graal’s Gambit + existing Blessed Tile Gambits make tile-based builds more consistent. Read our Spectral Piece Guide.

Builds Weakened

  • Templar-dependent builds – If your strategy relied on Templar’s guaranteed trigger, you need to pivot. Swap to the buffed 1/1 Gambits above.
  • Lich builds – Lich was nerfed in v1.2.0 and hasn’t recovered. Skip it unless you have specific synergy pieces.

Blitzking Boss Guide

Blitzking gets 2 actions per turn. Use the Wasted-Turn strategy to beat him. 3-phase flowchart + Gambit counter table.

Advertisement

Blitzking-TL;DR Check Boss Battle Guide for the full breakdown. The Boss Strategy Guide breaks this down in detail.

Don’t out-pace him. Make him waste his extra actions.

Every forced defense = 1 wasted action. Force 2 defenses per turn and his advantage is gone.

AWin rate went from 20%-75% after I stopped racing him and started wasting his turns.

Why Blitzking Kills Runs

TurnWhat Blitzking DoesWhat You Should Do
1Moves piece (aggressive)Deploy Knight to center
2Moves piece again OR plays GambitCreate a threat, don’t overcommit
3+Pressure builds 2x normalForce a defensive response

Key insight: Blitzking’s AI always addresses immediate threats first. Use this against him.

Counter Gambits-Pick 2

GambitCostEffect vs Blitzking
FreezeMediumCancels 1 of his 2 actions. Equal turns.
-Early CheckLowForces 1-2 defensive actions. Wastes the turn.
Board ShrinkMedium6x6-4x4. Extra moves matter less on small boards.
SwapLow-MedDisrupts his setup. Low value but disruptive.
Heal BoardLowOnly if he’s using Crumble (~30% of runs).

Must-have: Freeze + Early Check. Everything else is optional.

The Wasted-Turn Table

Your MoveBlitzking’s ResponseNet Result
Early CheckMust block (1 action)- Blitzking action
Knight ForkMust save 1 piece (1-2 actions)- to-
FreezeLoses 1 action entirelyEqual turns (!)
Check + ForkMust block check, fork still active- (both actions wasted)

When you waste 2+ actions/turn, Blitzking plays like a normal opponent. That’s when you win.

What NOT to Do

-MistakeWhy It Fails
Play slow setupsEvery setup turn = 2 free attacks for Blitzking
Ignore your King’s back rankBlitzking checkmates in 4 turns if exposed
Save Gambits “for later”Later never comes. Use Freeze on turn 1 if you have it.
Try to outpace himHe has 2 actions. You can’t out-speed him.
Pawn-heavy buildsPawns are too slow. Need immediate threats.
